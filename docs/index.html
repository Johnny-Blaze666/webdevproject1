Solution-oriented and inventive Information Security Manager with 19+ years of success leveraging technology to support diverse business needs. Combine in-depth experience in systems administration, project management, disaster recovery, and Information Security Operations with holistic and forward-thinking approach to evaluating technology needs and crafting unique security solutions. About Me

My skills

Oversee the Implementation of multiple Information Security solutions in partnership with IT and key business stakeholders. Write Policies, Standards and Procedures. Provide direction to team of Security Analysts as it relates to cyber security tools, security incident management and threat detection. Working knowledge of Information Security Frameworks such as NIST, CIS Controls Benchmarks, MITRE ATT&CK and map existing controls to frameworks. Identify Gaps in existing controls, recommend and implement solutions. Review existing solutions to ensure their value is being maximized. Manage daily security operations, incident escalations and response. Ensure SIEM correlations and escalations are actionable. Work closely with Governance, Risk and Compliance (GRC) team to create reports and implement server controls. Other activities include reviewing forensic analysis of information systems events and network flow data as part of incident analysis, as well as performing ad-hoc queries for collection of evidence in response to incidents or other investigations. .
